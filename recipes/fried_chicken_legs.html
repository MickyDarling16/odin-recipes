Air Fryer Chicken Legs Recipe

Description

Ingredients

  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 4 skin-on chicken drumsticks

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Spread 1 to 2 tablespoons mayonnaise (depending on taste) evenly over both slices of bread.
  3. Place two slices of tomato on one slice of bread and sprinkle with half of the salt and pepper.
  4. Add two more slices of tomato and sprinkle with remaining salt and pepper
  5. Place second slice of bread, mayonnaise side down, on the top.
  6. Slice and Enjoy!
